## ğŸ” Encryption Algorithm Details

**Rescue Cipher** (Arcium's choice):
- Algebraic cipher designed for MPC and zero-knowledge proofs
- Operates on finite fields (Rescue-XLIX)
- Efficient in circuit representation
- Resistant to algebraic attacks
- Optimized for Arcium's multi-party execution

**x25519 Key Exchange**:
- Elliptic curve Diffie-Hellman (ECDH)
- Curve25519 for key agreement
- Derives shared secret between client and MXE
- Fast, secure, and battle-tested

**Data Compression**:
- Payload compressed to uint128 words
- Padded to 16-byte blocks
- Minimizes on-chain storage costs
- Decompressed after decryption

## ğŸ¯ Why This Architecture?

**Current Hybrid Approach:**
1. **Speed**: Database queries much faster than scanning blockchain
2. **Cost**: Postgres storage cheaper than Solana rent
3. **UX**: Instant bet history without wallet signatures
4. **Privacy**: Better than fully public, worse than fully E2E

**Future Decentralized Approach:**
1. **Sovereignty**: Users own their data completely
2. **Censorship Resistance**: No centralized database to shut down
3. **Trust**: Don't trust Punt servers with your bets
4. **Composability**: Other apps can read encrypted bets (with permission)

**The Trade-Off:**
- Phase 1 prioritizes **UX and speed** for hackathon demo
- Phase 2+ prioritizes **privacy and decentralization** for production
